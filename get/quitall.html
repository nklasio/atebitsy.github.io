QuitAll

One tweak to quit them all

Sometimes you just need to clear all apps from your app switcher. But that can be a painful process if you haven't done it in a while. That is where QuitAll comes in handy.

QuitAll adds a a "Clear" button on top of your app switcher, once pressed it - you guessed it - clears all apps from the app switcher.

Full feature list:

  • Clear all apps
  • Don't clear music related apps
  • Change the buttons background color to black/white blur
  • Align the button to the left or right
  • Free
  • Open Source

The source code of the tweak is publicly available here: https://github.com/vanwijkdave/QuitAll

For more info about my upcoming tweaks, you can follow my twitter: https://twitter.com/davewijk

Are you busy working out or maybe can't reach your phone but still want to know newest & hottest notifications? Well, say no more! Blackout can help you with those problems. Your device will speak any incoming notifications of your favourite apps and even more you can choose your favourite accent, usage option, and many more!
Main Features ‚úÖ
- Speak any message within your local voice accent / with siri voices! (configurable)
- Speak any incoming message only when you are connected to Wired Headphones, Airpods, and more!
- Speak to only your specific bluetooth device (when connected)
- Set to only speak message from your chosen favourite applications.
- Set custom volume only when speaking the notification and revert back when done speaking.
- Add more information when speaking the notification, such as sender name, etc!
- Speak message even if the device is in "silent" mode.
- Many more!

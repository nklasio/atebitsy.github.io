ForwardNotifier

Jailbroken iOS tweak

To download, visit this
page on your iOS device.

ForwardNotifier has been made with the people who work at their desks in mind.

Have you ever received a bunch of notifications only to go get your phone and see it’s ads? Then look no further, with this tweak you can see your notifications right on your desktop, so you can decide whether it’s important or not without having to grab your phone.

It will help you stay focused by not taking your phone at every little vibration you hear, and only pay attention to the things that matter.

This tweak does require extra software on the desktop’s end, so please, go to https://github.com/Greg0109/ForwardNotifier and follow the instructions to get your phone notifications on your desktop today!

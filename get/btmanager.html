BTManager+

Jailbroken iOS tweak

To download, visit this
page on your iOS device.

BTManager+ brings powerful management features to your Bluetooth devices.

Features:

  • Rename any Bluetooth device
  • Display a confirm alert when switching to the audio route to a specific device
  • Configure a default device and priority list for each application
  • More Coming Soon

Support:

Feel free to reach out to me for help or post an issue on the project GitHub

Compatibility

Supports iPhone, iPad, and iPod touch running iOS 13.0 - 13.7. Jailbreak required.
